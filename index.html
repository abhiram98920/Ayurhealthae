Common Questions

Your Concerns, Addressed

What happens in the first consultation?
Our doctors perform Nadi Pariksha (Pulse Diagnosis) to identify your Prakriti and current Dosha imbalance, followed by a personalized curative plan.
Is Ayurveda safe alongside Allopathy?
Yes, clinical Ayurveda can often be integrated with modern medicine. Our doctors will guide you on the safest protocols based on your specific medications.
Are there dietary restrictions during treatment?
Ayurveda considers food as medicine. Based on your condition, we provide a simple, practical healing dietary path.
How long does a typical Panchakarma program last?
Programs typically range from 7 to 21 days depending on your health goals and current condition. Our physicians will recommend the optimal duration after your initial assessment.
