General Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that starting a new healing journey can bring up many questions. Below, we have answered some of the most common doubts.

What should I expect during my first consultation?
Your first visit is all about understanding you deeply. Our expert Ayurvedic doctors will have a detailed, relaxed conversation with you about your health, daily routine, and past medical history. They will carefully check your pulse (Nadi Pariksha) to understand your unique body type (Dosha).
Are Ayurvedic treatments safe, and do they have side effects?
Yes, authentic Ayurvedic treatments are completely safe. Because we use only the purest, highest-quality herbal medicines and medicated oils, there are no harmful side effects. Our therapies are guided by clinical experts who ensure that your body heals gently and naturally.
How long will it take to see the results?
Ayurveda aims to permanently cure the root cause of the disease. Depending on how old or severe your ailment is, you may start feeling better in a few days, while complete healing may take a few weeks. Trusting the process brings lifelong rewards.
Do I need to follow a very strict diet during my treatment?
In Ayurveda, food is considered medicine. Our doctors will suggest a simple, nourishing diet plan that matches your body type (Prakriti). We do not believe in starving you; instead, we guide you on making healthy food choices that speed up your recovery.
Can I take Ayurvedic medicines along with your regular medicines?
Yes, in most cases, you can safely continue both. However, it is important to share your current medical prescriptions with our doctors. Our specialists will guide you on how to balance both, usually by keeping a simple time gap between them.
How can I book an appointment with your specialists?
Booking your visit is very easy. You can click the floating "Book Appointment" button on our website, send us a quick WhatsApp message, or call our clinic directly. Our staff will personally assist you in choosing a date and time.
